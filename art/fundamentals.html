Fundamentals of Art

Projects are organized in chronological order, from earliest to most recent.

Chronological Projects

Personal Playing Card

My own playing card design with a Greek pillar in the center, vines wrapping around it, and eyes floating in the air. I focused on clean lines and keeping the details balanced.

Line Landscape Drawing

A beach scene done only with lines and dots. I used thicker lines for the palm trunks and tighter line textures in the sand and water to show depth.

Linear Sculpture

A 3D sculpture built from pyramid shapes connected together. The angles shift as it moves across the base, and keeping it stable was the hardest part.

Pencil / Colored Pencil Shattered Drawing

A shattered-style drawing of an old PC, built mostly from triangles. I blended graphite to get smooth value shifts and kept the edges sharp.

Color Wheel

A hand-painted color wheel with smooth transitions between each color slice. A few brush strokes show through, so it has a slightly rough texture.

Color Scheme Watercolor Painting

A watercolor piece built around a C# logo, repeated in different color schemes. I tried to keep the washes even and the borders between shapes clean.

Scratchboard Animal

A scratchboard cat with lots of short scratch marks to build fur texture. The bright highlights around the face and paws were my favorite part.
