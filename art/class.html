Class Projects

Projects are organized in chronological order, from earliest to most recent.

Fundamentals of Art · Chronological

Personal Playing Card

A custom playing card design that focuses on line quality, pattern, and symmetry. I concentrated on balance and contrast while creating a unique central motif.

Line Landscape Drawing

A line-only landscape using varied line weight and texture to suggest depth. I practiced spacing, rhythm, and detail to create a calm beach scene.

Linear Sculpture

A 3D linear sculpture built with repeating triangular forms. The main challenge was structural stability while keeping the overall form dynamic.

Pencil / Colored Pencil Shattered Drawing

A fractured, geometric drawing that emphasizes value changes across planes. I focused on clean edges and gradual shading to show depth.

Color Wheel

A hand-painted color wheel demonstrating primary, secondary, and tertiary colors. This project strengthened my understanding of mixing and transitions.

Color Scheme Watercolor Painting

A watercolor painting organized by color schemes and geometric shapes. I worked on even washes and controlling water to keep edges crisp.

Scratchboard Animal

A scratchboard animal study focused on texture and strong contrast. This project taught me to plan carefully since mistakes are permanent.
