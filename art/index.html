About The Artist

My name is Nathan Hornby, and I’m 17 years old. I enjoy drawing, painting, and building forms that explore beyond normal drawing standards. My main form of art is programming, in which I spend most my time doing. Each line of code to me is like a brush stroke. Eventually you either have a beautiful masterpiece... or garbage. Programming is my primary medium. I see code as a personal form of expression, not just a technical skill. Writing code allows me to think, experiment, and translate abstract ideas into something tangible and functional. Each line reflects a decision I make, and the final result mirrors my mindset, discipline, and problem-solving process.
