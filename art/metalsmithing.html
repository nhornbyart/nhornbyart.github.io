Metalsmithing and Jewelry Design 1

Projects are organized in chronological order, from earliest to most recent.

Chronological Projects

Sweat Solder

My first solder project. I focused on getting a clean join and cutting out shapes. This one was inspired by Bioshock.

Jump Rings

Three brass rings. This was practice for ring soldering, and the seams turned out clean, almost undetectable.

Leaves and Flowers

My least favorite. The leaves and flowers look a bit rough, and the texture didn’t come out as clean as I wanted, but again I didn't use my time too wisely.

Domed Ring

A domed ring that came out with good roundness. The finish is a bit rough, but the shape turned out okay. This was to practice rings.

Signature Ring

Intertwined brass, copper, and silver rings. I made two, and this is the one that turned out really clean. Unironically I made this one before I made the domed ring.
